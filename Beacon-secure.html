How It Works

1. Digital Identity Creation: Tourists receive blockchain-verified digital IDs at entry points with integrated KYC and emergency contacts.

2. Smart Monitoring: AI algorithms continuously analyze movement patterns, location data, and behavior to calculate real-time safety scores.

3. Proactive Alerts: Geo-fencing technology triggers warnings when entering high-risk zones, with graduated response protocols.

4. Emergency Response: Instant panic button activation dispatches help to nearest police units with GPS coordinates and automated documentation.

5. Authority Dashboard: Real-time visualization tools help tourism departments and police monitor tourist clusters and manage incidents efficiently.
